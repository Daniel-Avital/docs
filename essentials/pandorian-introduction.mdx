## Welcome to Pandorian

As engineering organizations grow, maintaining consistent standards becomes harder. Teams fragment, knowledge drifts, and the same issues surface in code review again and again. AI coding tools accelerate output — and accelerate the problem.

Pandorian is a codebase governance platform for engineering leaders. Write your guidelines in plain English or export them from your existing documentation — Pandorian enforces them across every repository and every pull request, on every line of code, whether it was written by a developer or an AI agent.

---

## Why Pandorian Exists

Engineering leaders set the standards. Enforcing them is a different problem.

Guidelines live in Confluence pages, Slack threads, and the heads of your most senior engineers. Linters cover syntax. Code review catches some things, some of the time. But as codebases grow and AI accelerates output, the gap between what you expect and what ships keeps widening.

Pandorian exists to close that gap — turning your existing standards into real-time enforcement across every pull request and every repository, without asking developers to change how they work.

---

## Who Pandorian is For

Pandorian is built for engineering leaders who define the standards for the developer org — CTOs, VPs of Engineering, VP Platform, Chief Architects, and CISOs leading large teams and codebases.

If your organization has standards that aren't consistently enforced, or if the same violations keep appearing in code review, Pandorian gives you the enforcement layer you've been missing.
